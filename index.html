Repeat After Me!

type speed level [chord]

Instructions

The premise of this game is to repeat the sequence presented by the computer. The sequence starts with just one item and increases in both length and speed incrementally. The sequence must be repeated at least as fast as the computer presents it. The sequence can be repeated using the buttons in the play area or by using the a, s, d, and f keys. I playing using sounds as the sequence item, the lowest tone is triggered by a, followed by s and d and then f triggers the highest tone.
